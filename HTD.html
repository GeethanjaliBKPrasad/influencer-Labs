Hire Train Deploy Programs

Hire: Based on the business needs and objectives of our clients, we hire apprentices/freshers/candidates.

Train: We provide relevant training to our hires to instill soft skills and job specific skill sets.

Deploy: Deploy fully trained candidates for the client’s projects.

Support: To increase the success rate of our Hire-Train-Deploy model, we support both clients and candidates throughout the process and post deployment.

HTD - Approach

Our HTD model is robust, flexible, and cost-effective, yet simple. It includes the following steps-

We source suitable candidates as per JD through multiple channels and shortlist them. Our expert trainers train them for a period of 6 to 8 weeks as per the requirements set by our clients.

We continuously monitor and support the trainees and prepare them to face an interview with the client.

Interim Attendance and participation in assignments and Assessments are validated Upon completion of the final evaluation, we set up interview rounds with the clients. Post the interviews, the client selects candidates who are suitable to be deployed
